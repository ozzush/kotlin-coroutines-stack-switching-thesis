\chapter{Conclusion and current state}
\label{ch:conclusion}

This thesis presented an alternative implementation of Kotlin Coroutines for Kotlin/Wasm based on the
WebAssembly Stack-switching proposal. The implementation maps Kotlin coroutine primitives to
Wasm Stack-switching instructions, replacing the state-machine transformation approach used by the
existing Kotlin/JS-based implementation. The evaluation showed measurable improvements in binary size,
but it also has big regressions in performance across the tested benchmarks.

\section{Current state}

The Stack-switching proposal is currently at Phase~3 of the WebAssembly standardization process~\cite{wasm-proposals},
meaning it is not yet available in most VMs. The development of Kotlin Coroutines with Stack-switching is
nevertheless sustained by JetBrains, and at the moment of writing it, the implementation is being prepared for release
as an experimental feature under a compiler flag. JetBrains is actively collaborating with the V8 team on ways to
improve Stack-switching implementation~\cite{wasm-stack-switching-repo}.
